<p><p>The System-in-Package (SiP) market consists of three types: 2D IC, 2.5D IC, and 3D IC. The 2D IC market refers to traditional integrated circuits (ICs) that are placed on a single layer. In the 2.5D IC market, multiple ICs are stacked vertically using an interposer, enabling faster speeds and reduced power consumption. The 3D IC market takes stacking a step further, allowing multiple layers of ICs to be interconnected, offering high functionality in a small footprint. These advancements in SiP technology cater to the growing demand for smaller, faster, and more efficient electronic devices.</p></p>
