<p><p>Water dispersible ink market types are categorized based on the type of resin used in the formulation. Resins commonly used include Acrylic, Polyester, Maleic, Polyurethane Resin, and Phenolic Resin. Acrylic resin provides good adhesion and flexibility. Polyester resin offers excellent chemical resistance and durability. Maleic resin enhances the ink's wetting properties. Polyurethane resin provides high abrasion resistance. Phenolic resin offers excellent heat resistance and adhesion to various substrates. These different resin types cater to various printing applications in the market.</p></p>
