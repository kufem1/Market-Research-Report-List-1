<p><p>Performance Monitoring Module markets include Optical Power Meters (OPM), Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDR), and Optical Loss Test Sets (OLTS). OPMs measure the strength of optical signals, OTDRs analyze the integrity of fiber optic cables, and OLTSs test the overall attenuation of a fiber optic link. These modules are essential for ensuring the efficiency and reliability of fiber optic networks. Companies in the telecommunications, data centers, and IT industries rely on these tools to maintain and optimize their network infrastructure.</p></p>
