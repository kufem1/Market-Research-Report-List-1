<p><p>Activated carbon derived from wood can be categorized into three main types based on their physical form: powdered activated carbon, granular activated carbon, and others. Powdered activated carbon is finely ground and has a high surface area, making it ideal for applications such as water treatment and gas purification. Granular activated carbon consists of larger particles, allowing for more contact time with gases and liquids. The "others" category may include speciality products like activated carbon fibers or pellets with unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
