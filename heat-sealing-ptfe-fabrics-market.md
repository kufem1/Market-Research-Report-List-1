<p><strong>The Heat Sealing PTFE Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat sealing PTFE fabrics are used in various industries for their heat resistance and non-stick properties. Porous PTFE fabrics are permeable, allowing for air and liquid to pass through, making them ideal for filtration applications. On the other hand, non-porous PTFE fabrics do not allow for any substance to pass through, making them suitable for applications requiring a waterproof barrier. Both types of PTFE fabrics are commonly used in industries such as food packaging, medical, and chemical processing.</p></p>
