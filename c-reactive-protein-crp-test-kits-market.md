<p><p>The C Reactive Protein (CRP) Test Kits Market includes various types such as Immunoturbidimetric, ELISA, CLIA, and others. Immunoturbidimetric Kits are based on the principle of measuring changes in light scattering caused by antigen-antibody reactions. ELISA Kits use enzyme-linked antibodies to detect CRP levels, while CLIA Kits use chemiluminescence for detection. Other types may include rapid diagnostic tests or molecular assays. Each type offers different advantages in terms of sensitivity, specificity, and ease of use for CRP testing.</p></p>
