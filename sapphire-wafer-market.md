<p><p>Sapphire wafers are categorized based on the orientation of their crystal planes, namely A-plane, C-plane, and R-plane. A-plane wafers have a unique crystal orientation that results in higher electron mobility, making them ideal for high-frequency and high-power electronic applications. C-plane wafers are commonly used in LED manufacturing due to their optimal light emission properties. R-plane wafers have a unique combination of properties that make them suitable for specific applications in the semiconductor industry. Each type of sapphire wafer caters to different market demands and applications.</p></p>
