<p><p>A throat swab, also known as a throat culture or throat test, is a medical procedure used to collect samples from the back of the throat for laboratory testing. These swabs are then sent to the laboratory to identify and test for various pathogens, including bacteria and viruses, that could be causing an infection or illness.</p><p>The throat swab market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years, and this trend is expected to continue in the coming years.
