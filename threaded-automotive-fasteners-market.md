<p><p>Threaded automotive fasteners come in various types of materials to suit different requirements. Iron material fasteners are strong and durable, while aluminum material fasteners are lightweight and resistant to corrosion. Brass material fasteners offer good electrical conductivity, while nickel material fasteners provide high levels of strength and resistance to extreme temperatures. Stainless steel material fasteners are known for their corrosion resistance and durability. Plastic material fasteners are lightweight and cost-effective, but may not be as strong as metal alternatives.</p></p>
