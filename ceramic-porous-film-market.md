<p><p>Ceramic porous films are classified into three main types: plate type, tubular type, and multichannel type. Plate type ceramic films are thin sheets with a uniform porous structure, tubular type films are cylindrical in shape with porous walls, and multichannel films have multiple channels for improved fluid flow. Each type of ceramic porous film is designed for specific applications, such as filtration, separation, and catalysis, based on their unique structure and properties.</p></p>
