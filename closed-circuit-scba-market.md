<p><p>Closed-circuit SCBAs are classified into two types based on the pressure system used: High Pressure Open-Circuit SCBA and Low Pressure Open-Circuit SCBA. High Pressure Open-Circuit SCBAs utilize compressed air cylinders at high pressure levels to supply breathable air to users. On the other hand, Low Pressure Open-Circuit SCBAs utilize cylinders with lower pressure levels. Both types of SCBAs are designed to provide users with a continuous supply of breathable air in hazardous environments, ensuring their safety and protection.</p></p>
