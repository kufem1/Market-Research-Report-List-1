<p><p>Pre-coated plates are used in cell culture experiments to provide a surface for cells to adhere to. There are two main types of pre-coated plates: extracellular and intracellular. Extracellular pre-coated plates have a surface coating that mimics the extracellular matrix, promoting cell attachment and growth. On the other hand, intracellular pre-coated plates have a coating that facilitates the delivery of molecules into cells for studies on intracellular processes. Both types of pre-coated plates play a crucial role in cell culture research.</p></p>
