<p><p>Ammonium Phosphate market is segmented into two major types: Monoammonium Phosphate (MAP) and Diammonium Phosphate (DAP). MAP is a fertilizer that contains higher concentrations of nitrogen and phosphate, making it suitable for crops that require more nitrogen during the early stages of growth. DAP, on the other hand, has a balanced ratio of nitrogen and phosphate, making it ideal for crops that need a more balanced nutrient supply throughout their growth cycle. These two types cater to different agricultural needs and play a crucial role in enhancing crop yield and quality.</p></p>
