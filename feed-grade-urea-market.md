<p><p>Feed grade urea is a type of urea specifically designed for use in animal feeds. It comes in two main forms: granules and powder. Granular feed grade urea is formed into small pellets or beads for easier handling and mixing into feed rations. Powder feed grade urea is finely ground for quick dissolving and mixing.
