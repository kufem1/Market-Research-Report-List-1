<p><p>Knee Orthosis (KO) Market includes two main types of orthotics: Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. Dynamic Orthotics are designed to provide support and stability to the knee joint while allowing some degree of movement. This type of orthosis is commonly used in sports injuries and rehabilitation. On the other hand, Static Orthotics immobilize the knee joint and limit its range of motion. This type of orthosis is often used in cases of severe knee injuries or post-operative recovery. Both types play a significant role in the treatment and management of knee conditions.</p></p>
