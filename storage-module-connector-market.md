Dual In-line connectors have two rows of connecting pins, providing a stable and secure connection. Single In-line connectors have a single row of pins, making them more compact and suitable for smaller devices. Others refer to a variety of connector types that do not fit into the Dual In-line or Single In-line categories, offering more specialized solutions for different storage module needs.</p></p>
