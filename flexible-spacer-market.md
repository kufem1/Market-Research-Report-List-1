<p><ul><li>Plastic Spacers</li><li>Plastic/Metal Hybrid Spacers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible spacer market includes plastic spacers and plastic/metal hybrid spacers. Plastic spacers are made entirely of plastic, offering insulation and flexibility. They are lightweight and cost-effective. Plastic/metal hybrid spacers combine the benefits of both materials, providing improved strength and durability. They are suitable for applications requiring higher load-bearing capacities. Both types of spacers are widely used in construction, automotive, and aerospace industries for thermal insulation, vibration damping, and structural support requirements.</p></p>
