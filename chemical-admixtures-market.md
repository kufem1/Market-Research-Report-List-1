<p><strong>The Chemical Admixtures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Entrainers</li><li>Water Reducers</li><li>Set Retarders</li><li>Set Accelerators</li><li>Superplasticizers</li><li>Specialty Admixtures</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical admixtures market is divided into different types based on their functions. Air entrainers are used to create small air bubbles in concrete for increased durability. Water reducers help in reducing the water content without affecting the workability of concrete. Set retarders slow down the setting time of concrete, while set accelerators speed up the process. Superplasticizers improve workability and strength. Specialty admixtures cater to specific needs, while others include a mix of various functions such as corrosion inhibitors and shrinkage reducers.</p></p>
