<p><p>Medical dental adhesives sealants market can be categorized into different types based on the materials used in their formulation. Cyanoacrylate adhesives are fast-acting and ideal for quick fixes and minor repairs. Polyethylene glycol (PEG) adhesives offer biocompatibility and flexibility in dental applications.
