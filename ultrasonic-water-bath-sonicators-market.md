<p><p>Ultrasonic water bath sonicators are used in various industries for applications such as sample preparation, cell disruption, and particle size reduction. There are two main types of ultrasonic water baths: circulating water baths and non-circulating water baths. Circulating water baths utilize a pump to circulate water throughout the bath, maintaining a consistent temperature throughout. Non-circulating water baths do not have a pump and rely on convection to distribute heat evenly. Both types offer precise temperature control and efficient ultrasonic wave transmission for effective sample processing.</p></p>
