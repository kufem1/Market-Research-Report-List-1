<p><p>Fatty alcohols are categorized into four main types based on the length of their carbon chain. Long chain fatty alcohols are those with 14 to 22 carbons, while short chain fatty alcohols have 4 to 12 carbons. Pure and midcut fatty alcohols fall in between the long and short chain categories. Lastly, higher chain fatty alcohols have more than 22 carbons. Each type of fatty alcohol has different characteristics and uses in various industries such as personal care products, detergents, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
