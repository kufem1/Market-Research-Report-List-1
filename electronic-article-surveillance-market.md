<p><p>Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) market includes various types of products like Hard Tags, Soft Tags, Deactivators or Detachers, Detection Systems, and Permanent Deactivation Tags. Hard Tags are durable and reusable, while Soft Tags are disposable and cost-effective. Deactivators or Detachers are devices used to remove or deactivate tags at the point of sale. Detection Systems alert staff of any unpaid items leaving the store. Permanent Deactivation Tags are tags that are deactivated at the point of sale and cannot be reactivated.</p></p>
