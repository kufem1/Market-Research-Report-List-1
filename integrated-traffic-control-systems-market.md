<p><p>Integrated Traffic Control Systems market includes three main types: Traffic Monitoring Systems, Traffic Control Systems, and Information Provision Systems. Traffic Control Systems manage traffic signals and regulate traffic flow to optimize efficiency. Information Provision Systems provide real-time information to drivers through various channels to improve navigation and reduce congestion.
