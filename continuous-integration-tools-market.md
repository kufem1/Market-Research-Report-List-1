<p><p>Continuous integration tools are software solutions used by development teams to automate code testing and integration. These tools can be categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CI tools are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing for scalability, easy deployment, and reduced infrastructure costs. On the other hand, on-premises CI tools are installed and operated within a company's own infrastructure, providing more control over data security and customization but requiring dedicated hardware and maintenance. Both types offer benefits and considerations depending on the organization's needs and requirements.</p></p>
