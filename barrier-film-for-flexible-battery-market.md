<p><ul><li>Multilayer Barrier (MLB) Film Technology</li><li>Thin Film Encapsulation (TFE) Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barrier film for flexible battery market types include Multilayer Barrier (MLB) Film Technology and Thin Film Encapsulation (TFE) Technology. MLB film technology involves the use of multiple layers of barrier films to provide enhanced protection against moisture and oxygen ingress. TFE technology, on the other hand, utilizes a thin layer of encapsulation material to protect the flexible battery from environmental factors. Both technologies are crucial for ensuring the longevity and performance of flexible batteries in various applications.</p></p>
