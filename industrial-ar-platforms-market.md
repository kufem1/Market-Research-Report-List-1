<p><p>Industrial AR platforms can be categorized into two types: Cloud Based and Web Based. Cloud Based platforms store data and applications in remote servers, allowing users to access them from any device with an internet connection. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and seamless updates without the need for heavy local hardware. On the other hand, Web Based platforms are accessible through web browsers, eliminating the need for downloading and installing separate applications. They offer convenience, as users can simply log in and access the platform from any device with a browser, making it user-friendly and easily accessible.</p></p>
