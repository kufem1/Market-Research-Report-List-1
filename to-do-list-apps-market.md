<p><p>To-do list apps can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based apps operate through the internet and store data on remote servers, allowing users to access their to-do lists from any device with an internet connection. Conversely, on-premises apps are installed on a local device or server and store data locally, enabling users to access their to-do lists only from that specific device. Each market type has its own set of advantages and considerations regarding data security, accessibility, and customization options.</p></p>
