<p><p>Pharma Grade PLA (Polylactic Acid) is categorized into different types based on the stereoisomeric composition. D-Type PLA consists of only the D isomer, L-Type PLA consists of only the L isomer, while DL-Type PLA consists of a racemic mixture of both D and L isomers. These different types cater to various pharmaceutical applications, as their stereoisomeric composition affects properties such as biodegradability, thermal stability, and mechanical strength. Therefore, the choice of PLA type in the pharma industry depends on specific requirements and desired properties for pharmaceutical products, such as drug delivery systems or implants.</p></p>
