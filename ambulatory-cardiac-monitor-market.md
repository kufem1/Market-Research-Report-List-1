<p><p>The ambulatory cardiac monitor market consists of various types of devices used for monitoring heart activity outside of medical facilities. Holter monitors are portable devices that record a patient's heart activity continuously for 24-48 hours. Event monitors are small, wearable devices that record heart activity on-demand when a patient experiences symptoms. Mobile cardiac telemetry systems use wearable sensors to transmit real-time heart data to a monitoring center remotely. Other types of ambulatory cardiac monitors include implantable loop recorders and smart clothing embedded with heart monitoring technology. These devices aid in the diagnosis and monitoring of cardiac conditions.</p></p>
