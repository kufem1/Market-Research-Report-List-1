<p><p>The water infrastructure equipment market consists of four main types: pipe, pumps, valves, and meters. Pipes are used to transport water from one location to another. Pumps are used to push water through the pipes. Valves control the flow and pressure of the water within the pipes. Meters are devices used to measure the amount of water being used. These components are essential for the proper functioning of water distribution systems and are key elements in providing clean and safe water to communities.</p></p>
