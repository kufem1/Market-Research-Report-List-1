<p><p>Locksmith software market offers two types of systems: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software is installed and operates on the buyer's local servers, providing total control over the software and data. It requires upfront hardware costs, regular updates, and maintenance. In contrast, a Cloud-Based system operates remotely on the vendor's servers, accessible through the internet. It allows for easy scalability, automatic updates, and reduced hardware costs. Users can access data and tools from anywhere, but security concerns arise as data is stored externally.</p></p>
