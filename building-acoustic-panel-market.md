<p><p>Building Acoustic Panel market is segmented into three types. Acoustic Membranes are thin, flexible materials that vibrate when sound waves hit them, absorbing noise. Resonators Panel consist of hollow chambers that vibrate at certain frequencies, cancelling out noise. Porous Material Panel are made of materials like foam or fiber that trap sound waves as they pass through, reducing noise levels. Each type of panel offers unique acoustic qualities, catering to different noise reduction needs in buildings.</p></p>
