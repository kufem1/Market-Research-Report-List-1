<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Image-Guided Focused Ultrasound (MRgFUS) System market includes three types of devices: Extracorporeal Devices, which use focused ultrasound waves from outside the body to treat tumors; Intracavitary Devices, which deliver ultrasound waves directly to a specific body cavity such as the uterus or prostate; and Interstitial Devices, which use thin needles to deliver ultrasound waves directly into the tumor. Each type of device offers different advantages and is used for specific applications in medical treatment.</p></p>
