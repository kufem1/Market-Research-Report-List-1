<p><p>A hypothermia cap, also known as a cooling cap, is a medical device worn on the head to reduce the risk of hair loss during chemotherapy treatment. This helps protect the hair follicles and reduce hair loss.</p><p>The Hypothermia Cap Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period.
