<p><p>Commercial Washer Extractor System market types include Open Pocket and Divided-Cylinder designs. Open Pocket systems feature a single drum where the laundry is agitated and extracted, making them suitable for washing large loads quickly. On the other hand, Divided-Cylinder systems have two separate compartments for washing and extracting, allowing for more delicate handling of fabrics and separate washing cycles. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different commercial laundry needs and preferences.</p></p>
