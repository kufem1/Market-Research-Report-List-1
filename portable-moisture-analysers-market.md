<p><p>Portable Moisture Analysers are used to measure moisture content in various materials. There are four main types of moisture analysers in the market - Microwave, Infrared, Loss on Drying (LOD), and Others. Microwave analysers use microwave radiation to determine moisture levels, while Infrared analysers use infrared light. Loss on Drying analysers measure moisture by heating a sample until all moisture is evaporated. The "Others" category includes other innovative technologies for moisture analysis. Each type of analyser has its own strengths and applications in moisture measurement.</p></p>
