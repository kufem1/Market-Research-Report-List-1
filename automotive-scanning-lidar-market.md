<p><p>Automotive scanning LiDAR market consists of three main types: short-range, mid-range, and long-range LiDAR systems. Short-range LiDAR is typically used for close proximity detection and obstacle avoidance within a few meters of the vehicle. Mid-range LiDAR covers a larger distance range of up to 100 meters and is commonly used for advanced driver assistance systems. Long-range LiDAR, on the other hand, can detect objects up to 200 meters away and is crucial for autonomous driving applications requiring a longer sensing range for safer navigation.</p></p>
