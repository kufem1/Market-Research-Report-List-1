<p><p>The ETFE architectural membrane market can be categorized into three types based on its transparency level: transparent, semi-transparent, and opaque. Transparent ETFE membranes allow natural light to pass through, creating a bright and open space. Semi-transparent membranes offer a balance between transparency and privacy, while opaque membranes provide complete privacy and are suitable for areas where light control is desired. Each type of membrane offers different aesthetic and functional qualities, catering to various architectural design needs.</p></p>
