<p><strong>The Refrigerant R134A Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tetrachlorethylene Process</li><li>Trichlorethylene Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigerant R134A is produced through two main processes: tetrachlorethylene and trichlorethylene. The tetrachlorethylene process involves reacting hydrogen fluoride with chloroform to create 1,1,1,2-tetrafluoroethane, which is then purified to obtain R134A. On the other hand, the trichlorethylene process involves reacting hydrogen fluoride with 1,1,1-trichloroethane to create R134A. Both processes are widely used in the market for producing this popular refrigerant, catering to different industry needs and preferences.</p></p>
