<p><p>Glass sheets market types include aluminosilicate, borosilicate, glass ceramic, quartz, soda lime, and other varieties. Aluminosilicate glass is known for its high strength and resistance to thermal shock, making it suitable for electronic devices and medical applications. Borosilicate glass is valued for its durability and resistance to chemicals, commonly used in laboratory glassware. Glass ceramic combines the properties of glass and ceramics, offering high thermal stability. Quartz glass is highly transparent and heat-resistant, ideal for optical and semiconductor industries. Soda lime glass is widely used in construction and packaging due to its low cost and versatility. Other types of glass sheets include specialty glasses tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
