<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Coating</li><li>Nanocoating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile Anti-Fog Coatings come in two main types, Hydrophilic Coating and Nanocoating. Hydrophilic Coating works by attracting water molecules to create a thin film that reduces fogging on the surface of the glass. Nanocoating, on the other hand, involves using nano-sized particles to create a barrier that repels water droplets and prevents fogging. Both types of coatings are effective in maintaining visibility during foggy conditions, but they differ in the mechanisms by which they achieve this outcome.</p></p>
