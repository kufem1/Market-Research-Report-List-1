<p><strong>The Inductive Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Inductive Type</li><li>Mutual Inductive Type</li><li>Eddy Current Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive sensors are classified into three main types based on their operating principles: self-inductive type, mutual inductive type, and eddy current type. </p><p>Self-inductive sensors generate a magnetic field on their own to detect changes in an object's conductivity. Mutual inductive sensors rely on the interaction between two coils to detect the presence of an object. Eddy current sensors use electromagnetic induction to measure changes in an object's electrical conductivity.</p><p>These different types cater to a wide range of industrial applications requiring accurate and reliable proximity sensing solutions.</p></p>
