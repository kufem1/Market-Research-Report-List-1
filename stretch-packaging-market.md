<p><p>Stretch packaging market types include Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE), Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), Poly Vinyl Chloride (PVC), and others. LLDPE is known for its high stretchability and tear resistance, making it ideal for securing irregularly shaped items. LDPE offers high clarity and flexibility, making it suitable for wrapping pallets. PVC provides excellent cling and puncture resistance. Other materials in the market include polyethylene terephthalate (PET) and polypropylene (PP), each with unique properties to meet various packaging needs.</p></p>
