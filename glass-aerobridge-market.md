<p><p>Glass aerobridges are structures used to connect terminals to aircraft, providing a sheltered passageway for passengers. There are two main types of glass aerobridges - those with an electromechanical elevation system and those with a hydraulic elevation system. The electromechanical system uses a combination of electrical and mechanical components to raise and lower the bridge, while the hydraulic system uses fluid power to achieve the same function. Both systems offer safe and convenient boarding for passengers while ensuring smooth operations at airports.</p></p>
