<p><p>A metal breather valve is a type of valve that is typically installed on storage tanks, process vessels, and pipelines to prevent excessive pressure or vacuum buildup. The primary driving factor for this growth is the increasing demand for storage tanks and process vessels in various industries. These industries require efficient venting systems to prevent potential damages caused by pressure or vacuum buildups. The metal breather valves offer excellent performance in harsh operating conditions, such as high temperature and high-pressure environments, making them ideal for various industrial applications.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements in metal breather valves, such as the development of advanced materials and designs, have increased their reliability and efficiency.
