<p><strong>The PPS Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Cross-linked Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPS resin market can be categorized into two main types based on their molecular structure: Linear Type and Cross-linked Type. Linear Type PPS resins have a linear molecular structure, making them easily moldable and offering high strength and chemical resistance. On the other hand, Cross-linked Type PPS resins have a more complex molecular structure due to the presence of cross-links between polymer chains, providing enhanced thermal and mechanical properties. Both types cater to different applications and industries in the polymer market.</p></p>
