<p><p>Dental resin composites are commonly classified into two main types: dimethacrylates and organically modified ceramics (OrMoCers). Dimethacrylates are versatile materials that offer excellent strength and durability, making them ideal for a wide range of dental applications. On the other hand, OrMoCers combine the aesthetics of ceramics with the flexibility of organic compounds, providing a more natural look and feel to dental restorations. Both types of resin composites are widely used in the dental industry for various treatments and procedures.</p></p>
