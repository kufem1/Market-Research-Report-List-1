<p><p>Fractionated coconut oil is a type of coconut oil that has been processed to remove long-chain triglycerides, leaving behind only the medium-chain triglycerides. This process results in a clear, odorless, and non-greasy oil that has a longer shelf life and a higher stability at lower temperatures than regular coconut oil. Fractionated coconut oil is commonly used in the cosmetics industry as a carrier oil for essential oils, as well as in skincare products due to its moisturizing and soothing properties.</p><p>The Fractionated Coconut Oil Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period. The key factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for natural and organic skincare products, the rising awareness about the benefits of coconut oil for skin and hair care, and the growing popularity of aromatherapy and essential oils.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Fractionated Coconut Oil Market is the increasing use of fractionated coconut oil in the food and beverage industry, particularly in the production of health and wellness products such as protein bars, smoothies, and functional beverages.
