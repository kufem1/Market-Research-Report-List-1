<p><p>Grain drying systems market includes two main types: stationary grain dryer and mobile grain dryer. Stationary grain dryers are fixed on-site facilities typically used for larger grain volumes, offering high capacity and efficiency. Mobile grain dryers, on the other hand, are portable units that can be easily transported to different locations, making them suitable for smaller farms or remote areas. Both types play a crucial role in preserving the quality of harvested grains by removing excess moisture before storage.</p></p>
