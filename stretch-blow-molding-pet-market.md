<p><strong>The Stretch Blow Molding PET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Process</li><li>Two-Stage Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stretch blow molding PET market includes two types - Single-Stage Process and Two-Stage Process. In Single-Stage Process, both the preform formation and bottle blowing are completed within the same machine, reducing production time and cost. This process is suitable for small to medium scale production. In Two-Stage Process, preforms are first created in a separate machine and then transferred to a blowing machine for forming the final product. This process is commonly used for large scale production due to its higher efficiency and flexibility in design options.</p></p>
