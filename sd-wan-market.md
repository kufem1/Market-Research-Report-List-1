<p><ul><li>Virtual appliance</li><li>Physical appliance</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SD-WAN market types refer to different forms in which software-defined wide area network solutions are delivered to customers. Virtual appliance involves software-only solutions that can be deployed on existing hardware, providing flexibility and scalability. Physical appliance consists of dedicated hardware devices that are pre-configured for SD-WAN, offering robust performance and security features. Hybrid market combines both virtual and physical appliances to offer customers a tailored solution that meets their specific requirements, blending the benefits of both approaches to maximize efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
