<p><strong>The Fire Retarding Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire retarding hydraulic fluids are classified into four types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are oil-in-water emulsions that contain 95% water and are commonly used in mining and forestry applications. HFB fluids are water glycol-based and offer improved lubrication properties for high-pressure systems. HFC fluids are water-free and environmentally friendly, making them suitable for sensitive areas. HFD fluids are synthetic esters that provide high thermal stability and fire resistance, ideal for extreme operating conditions.</p></p>
