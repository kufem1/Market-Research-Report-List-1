<p><p>Plastics extrusion market encompasses various types of plastics, including PVC, LDPE, HDPE, and PS. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile and commonly used in construction, healthcare, and automotive industries. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and is popular in packaging and agricultural applications. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is more rigid, making it suitable for pipes, bottles, and containers. PS (Polystyrene) is widely employed in food packaging and disposable products. Each type serves different purposes due to their unique physical and chemical properties, catering to diverse industries' requirements.</p></p>
