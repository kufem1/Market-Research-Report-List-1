<p><p>Well stimulation materials market includes various types such as proppants, base fluid materials, polymer gelling agents, biocides, and surfactants. Proppants are used to hold open fractures created during stimulation. Base fluid materials are used as carriers for chemicals. Polymer gelling agents are additives that enhance viscosity of fluids. Biocides are chemicals that control bacteria growth. Surfactants reduce surface tension of fluids. Each of these materials play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of well stimulation operations.</p></p>
