<p><p>Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is a type of polyethylene commodity plastic. There are three main market types of LLDPE based on the comonomer used during the polymerization process. C4-LLDPE refers to LLDPE made with a butene comonomer, C6-LLDPE is made with a hexene comonomer, and C8-LLDPE is made with an octene comonomer. The choice of comonomer influences the properties of the LLDPE, such as strength, flexibility, and transparency, making each market type suitable for different applications.</p></p>
