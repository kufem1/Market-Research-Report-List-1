<p><p>Semiconductor encapsulation materials market can be categorized into two types: epoxy-based materials and non-epoxy based materials. Epoxy-based materials are commonly used due to their strong adhesive properties and high resistance to heat and chemicals. On the other hand, non-epoxy based materials such as silicone and polyurethane offer flexibility, good thermal conductivity, and moisture resistance. Both types of materials play a crucial role in protecting semiconductor devices from external environmental factors and ensuring their reliability and longevity.</p></p>
