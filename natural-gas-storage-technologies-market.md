<p><p>Natural gas storage technologies are used to store natural gas for future use. Liquefied natural gas (LNG) storage involves converting natural gas into a liquid form by cooling it to extremely low temperatures. This allows for easier transportation and storage. Compressed natural gas (CNG) storage involves compressing natural gas to high pressures, reducing its volume and making it easier to store in tanks.
