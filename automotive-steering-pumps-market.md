<p><p>Automotive steering pumps are crucial components in vehicles that help to assist the driver in steering the vehicle. There are three main types of steering pumps in the market: Mechanical Hydraulic Power Steering, Electronic Hydraulic Power Steering, and Electric Power Steering. Mechanical Hydraulic Power Steering uses a hydraulic system to assist in steering, while Electronic Hydraulic Power Steering combines electronic sensors with hydraulic power. Electric Power Steering, on the other hand, uses an electric motor to assist in steering, offering a more efficient and responsive driving experience.</p></p>
