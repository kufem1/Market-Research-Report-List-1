<p><p>Hazelnut oil comes in two main market types: Roasted Hazelnut Oil and Cold Pressed Hazelnut Oil. Roasted Hazelnut Oil is made by roasting hazelnuts before extracting the oil, which gives it a rich, nutty flavor. This type of oil is commonly used in cooking and baking for its distinctive taste. On the other hand, Cold Pressed Hazelnut Oil is made by pressing raw hazelnuts at low temperatures, preserving more nutrients and flavor. It is typically used in skincare products and as a dietary supplement.</p></p>
