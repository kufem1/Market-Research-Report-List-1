<p><strong>The Audio Crossovers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio crossovers are devices used in audio systems to divide the audio signal into different frequency ranges for different speakers or drivers. Active crossovers are electronic devices that use built-in amplifiers to split the signal, offering more precise control over the audio output. They are commonly used in professional audio systems. On the other hand, passive crossovers are simple circuits that use passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to split the signal, requiring separate amplifiers for each driver. They are typically used in consumer-grade audio systems.</p></p>
