<p><ul><li>Automatic Transmission System</li><li>Manual Transmission System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>All-terrain vehicle transmission systems can be divided into two main types: automatic transmission systems and manual transmission systems. Automatic transmission systems use a torque converter to shift gears automatically, requiring less input from the driver. On the other hand, manual transmission systems require the driver to manually shift gears using a clutch and gearbox. Each system has its advantages and disadvantages, with automatic transmission systems offering convenience and ease of use, while manual transmission systems offer more control and efficiency.</p></p>
