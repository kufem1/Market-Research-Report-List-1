<p><p>Pressure sensitive aluminum tape is available in two main market types: single coated tape and double coated tape. </p><p>Single coated tape has adhesive applied to only one side of the aluminum foil, making it suitable for a wide range of applications such as joining and sealing. </p><p>Double coated tape, on the other hand, has adhesive applied to both sides of the aluminum foil, making it ideal for applications where both sides of the tape need to adhere to different surfaces simultaneously. Both types offer strong adhesion and durability for various industrial and commercial uses.</p></p>
