<p><p>The food waste disposer market consists of two main types: pulverizing type and drying type. The pulverizing type disposer operates by grinding the food waste into small particles, which can easily flow through the plumbing system. On the other hand, the drying type disposer removes moisture from the food waste, reducing its weight and volume. Both types of disposers offer efficient solutions for managing food waste in households and commercial settings, ensuring proper disposal and reducing strain on waste management systems.</p></p>
