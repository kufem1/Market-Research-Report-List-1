<p><p>Prefilled syringes are a convenient and efficient way to administer drugs or vaccines. The market for prefilled syringes can be broadly classified into two types: glass prefilled syringes and polymer prefilled syringes. Glass prefilled syringes are made of borosilicate glass and have been widely used for their durability and chemical resistance. On the other hand, polymer prefilled syringes are made of plastic materials such as cyclic olefin copolymers and offer advantages like reduced breakage risk and improved drug compatibility. Both types cater to the growing demand for safe and easy-to-use drug delivery systems.</p></p>
