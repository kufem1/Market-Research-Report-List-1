<p><p>Ceramic fiber paper comes in various thicknesses, including 1mm, 2mm, 3mm, and others. The different types cater to specific needs and applications. The 1mm paper is thin and flexible, suitable for lightweight insulation and gasket applications. The 2mm paper provides slightly more insulation and can withstand higher temperatures.
