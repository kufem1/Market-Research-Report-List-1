<p><strong>The Disinfectants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidising Disinfectants</li><li>Non-oxidising Disinfectants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disinfectants are widely classified into two main types in the market: oxidising disinfectants and non-oxidising disinfectants. Oxidising disinfectants work by releasing oxygen radicals that attack the cell walls of microorganisms, while non-oxidising disinfectants work by disrupting the cell membranes. Both types are effective in killing bacteria, viruses, and fungi, but they differ in their mechanisms of action. Oxidising disinfectants are often more powerful and quick-acting, while non-oxidising disinfectants are known for their longer-lasting effects.</p></p>
