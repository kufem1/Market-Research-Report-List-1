<p><strong>The Acrylic Waterproof Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalline</li><li>Water Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic waterproof paint market types consist of crystalline and water emulsion. Crystalline waterproof paint forms crystals within the pores of the substrate, blocking water penetration. On the other hand, water emulsion waterproof paint uses water as a medium for carrying the paint particles, which then form a protective barrier against water. Both types of paints are commonly used for waterproofing surfaces such as concrete, masonry, and wood, providing durable and long-lasting protection against moisture.</p></p>
