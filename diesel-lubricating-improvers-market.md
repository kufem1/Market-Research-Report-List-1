<p><ul><li>Acidic Lubricating Improver</li><li>Non-Acidic Lubricating Improver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diesel Lubricating Improvers can be classified into two main types - Acidic Lubricating Improver and Non-Acidic Lubricating Improver. Acidic Lubricating Improvers typically contain additives that help to neutralize the acidic components present in the engine oil, preventing corrosion and wear on engine parts. On the other hand, Non-Acidic Lubricating Improvers work by enhancing the lubricating properties of the engine oil without the use of acidic additives. Both types of lubricating improvers are crucial in maintaining the longevity and efficiency of diesel engines.</p></p>
