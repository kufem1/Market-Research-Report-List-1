<p><ul><li>Watertight</li><li>Non-Watertight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deck hatches are available in two main types in the market - watertight and non-watertight. Watertight deck hatches are designed to prevent water from entering the ship or boat, providing a secure seal to protect against leaks and flooding. On the other hand, non-watertight deck hatches are not designed to provide a watertight seal, and may be used in areas where water exposure is not a concern. Both types of deck hatches serve different purposes based on the specific needs of the vessel.</p></p>
