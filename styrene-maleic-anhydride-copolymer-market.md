<p><p>Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer (SMA) has two main types - A-SMA and R-SMA. A-SMA is an amorphous copolymer known for its excellent adhesive properties, high heat resistance, and good compatibility with different materials. It is widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, and construction. On the other hand, R-SMA is a rubber-modified copolymer that offers improved impact resistance and flexibility. It is commonly used in applications where toughness and durability are essential, such as in the production of impact-resistant plastics and automotive parts.</p></p>
