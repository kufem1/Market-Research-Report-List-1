<p><p>Anaerobic Biological Treatment Technology market includes two main types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type systems involve feeding a specific amount of wastewater into a reactor where it is treated over a period of time before being discharged. Continuous type systems, on the other hand, involve continuous feeding of wastewater into a reactor, which is constantly treated and discharged. Both types offer effective and efficient ways to treat organic waste and produce biogas in anaerobic conditions.</p></p>
