<p><p>Scraper attachments are diverse tools used for various purposes. Knife handle attachments are designed to provide a comfortable grip and control during scraping tasks. Blade attachments serve as the main scraping component, effectively removing debris or material from surfaces. Connecting screw attachments securely join the blade to the handle and ensure stability. Whetstone attachments are used to sharpen and maintain the blade's cutting edge. Lastly, triangular file attachments help in shaping and refining the blade. Each of these attachments plays a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of scraping tasks.</p></p>
