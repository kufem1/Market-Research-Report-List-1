<p><p>Colored tattoo inks can be categorized into two main types: mineral pigment and organic pigment. Mineral pigment inks are derived from minerals like iron oxide, titanium dioxide, and nickel, providing vibrant and long-lasting colors. Organic pigment inks are made from plant-based ingredients like carbon black, giving a wide range of colors and a more natural look. Both types of inks are popular choices among tattoo artists and enthusiasts for their durability and color variety.</p></p>
