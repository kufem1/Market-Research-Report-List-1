<p><ul><li>Analog X-Ray</li><li>Digital X-Ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral X-ray machines are classified into two main types: Analog X-ray machines and Digital X-ray machines. Analog X-ray machines use traditional film processing methods to capture images, which are then developed and displayed on physical film. In contrast, Digital X-ray machines utilize electronic sensors to capture images, which are then processed and displayed on a computer screen. Digital X-ray machines offer advantages such as faster image processing, easier storage and retrieval of images, and the ability to manipulate and enhance images for better diagnosis.</p></p>
