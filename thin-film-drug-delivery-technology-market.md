<p><p>Thin-film drug delivery technology market comprises various types of drug delivery mechanisms. Oral thin films are dissolvable strips placed on the tongue or buccal mucosa for drug absorption. Transdermal films deliver drugs through the skin and are commonly used for pain relief or hormone replacement therapies. Other types include sublingual films, mucoadhesive buccal tablets, and ocular films. These innovative technologies offer advantages like convenience, ease of administration, increased patient compliance, and controlled drug release, making them a promising alternative to traditional drug delivery methods.</p></p>
