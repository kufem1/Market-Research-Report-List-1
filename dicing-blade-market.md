<p><p>Dicing blades are widely used in the semiconductor industry for cutting and shaping materials like silicon wafers. There are two main types of dicing blades: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades have a central hub that connects the blade to the dicing saw, providing stability and better cutting performance. On the other hand, hubless dicing blades do not have a central hub, allowing for faster and more precise cuts. Both types have their advantages and are used based on specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
