<p><ul><li>Teflon Coated</li><li>Anodized Aluminum Coated</li><li>Enameled Iron Coated</li><li>Ceramic Coating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonstick cooker market consists of several types of coatings to prevent food from sticking to the cooking surface. Teflon Coated cookware uses a nonstick coating made of polytetrafluoroethylene. Anodized Aluminum Coated cookware is aluminum that has been treated to create a hard, nonstick surface. Enameled Iron Coated cookware has a layer of enamel over cast iron. Ceramic Coating cookware is made with a ceramic nonstick surface. Other types of coatings may include titanium or diamond-infused materials for added durability and nonstick properties.</p></p>
