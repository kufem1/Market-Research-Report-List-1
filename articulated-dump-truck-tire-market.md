<p><p>Pneumatic tires are the most common type used in articulated dump trucks as they provide a cushioning effect which is ideal for off-road and rough terrain applications. Solid tires are known for their durability and resistance to punctures, making them suitable for extreme conditions. Polyurethane tires offer a combination of the benefits of both pneumatic and solid tires, providing a smooth ride while being resistant to wear and tear. Each type of tire has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the dump truck operation.</p></p>
