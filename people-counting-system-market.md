<p><p>People Counting System Market Types refer to the various technologies used for counting people in a given space. IR Beam technology utilizes infrared beams to detect the presence of individuals passing through the beam. Thermal Imaging technology detects heat signatures emitted by people to count them accurately. Video Based systems use cameras to analyze video footage and count people based on their movements. Other types may include wireless device tracking or sound wave analysis. These systems are used in various public spaces like malls, airports, and public transportation to monitor foot traffic and optimize operations.</p></p>
