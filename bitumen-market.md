<p><strong>The Bitumen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Bitumen</li><li>Petroleum Bitumen</li><li>Coal-Tar Pitch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bitumen is commonly classified into three main types: natural bitumen, petroleum bitumen, and coal-tar pitch. Natural bitumen is derived from natural crude oil deposits, while petroleum bitumen is produced through the refining of crude oil. Coal-tar pitch, on the other hand, is obtained through the distillation of coal tar. Each type of bitumen has its own unique properties and applications, with natural bitumen being commonly used in road construction, petroleum bitumen in roofing and waterproofing, and coal-tar pitch in the production of coatings and sealants.</p></p>
