<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market includes two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type sensors. Hall Type sensors use the Hall Effect to detect changes in magnetic fields and are primarily used for accurate speed measurement. Magnetic Electric Type sensors, on the other hand, use a magnetic coil to generate an electric signal based on wheel rotation, providing reliable speed data. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring precise monitoring of wheel speed in electric vehicles for safety and performance optimization.</p></p>
