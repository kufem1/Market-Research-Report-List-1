<p><p>Dental X-ray film processors market is divided into two main types: Intraoral film processors and Extraoral film processors. Intraoral film processors are designed to process smaller dental X-ray films that are placed inside the patient's mouth, providing detailed images of individual teeth and small areas. Extraoral film processors, on the other hand, are used to process larger X-ray films that capture images of the entire mouth, jaw, or skull. Both types of processors play a crucial role in producing high-quality dental X-ray images for diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
