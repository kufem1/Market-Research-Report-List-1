<p><p>Camera remote controls come in two main types: wireless remote control and cable remote control. Wireless remote controls use radio waves or infrared signals to trigger the camera's shutter from a distance, making them convenient for capturing self-portraits or group shots. Cable remote controls connect to the camera via a cord and allow for precise control over the shutter, making them ideal for long exposure shots or studio photography. Both types offer photographers greater flexibility and creative control over their images.</p></p>
