<p><p>Orthopedic connectors market can be classified into two types: non-absorbable and absorbable. Non-absorbable connectors are made of materials that do not degrade or dissolve over time, providing long-term stability and strength. On the other hand, absorbable connectors are designed to degrade and get absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. Both types of connectors offer their own advantages and are utilized based on the specific needs of the patient, surgical procedure, and surgeon's preference.</p></p>
