<p><p>The insulin preparation market consists of three main types: fast-acting, intermediate-acting, and long-acting insulin. Fast-acting insulin is designed to start working quickly after injection, helping to control blood sugar levels during meals. Intermediate-acting insulin takes a bit longer to start working and lasts for a longer duration, helping to regulate blood sugar levels between meals. Lastly, long-acting insulin provides a steady release of insulin throughout the day, helping to maintain blood sugar levels over an extended period. Each type of insulin serves different purposes in managing diabetes.</p></p>
