<p><p>The antibody market includes different types of antibodies such as IgM, IgG, IgA, and others. IgM antibodies are the first line of defense produced by the immune system upon encountering a new infection. IgG antibodies are the most common type found in blood and tissues, providing long-term immunity and protection against recurring infections. IgA antibodies are primarily present in bodily fluids like saliva and breast milk, protecting against infections in mucosal surfaces. Other antibodies, such as IgD and IgE, have specific roles in immune responses and allergies, respectively. These varied types of antibodies cater to different aspects of immune defense and contribute to the overall antibody market.</p></p>
