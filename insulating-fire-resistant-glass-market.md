<p><p>Insulating fire resistant glass market types include sandwich composite, wire fire, special fire, and hollow fire. Sandwich composite glass consists of layers of glass and insulating materials to provide fire protection. Wire fire glass has embedded wires that hold the glass together even when exposed to high temperatures. Special fire glass is specially treated to resist fire and heat. Hollow fire glass is filled with insulating materials to enhance its fire resistance properties. Each type offers unique features to meet different fire protection needs.</p></p>
