<p><p>Nylon monofilament market is segmented into two main types: Nylon 6 and Nylon 66. Nylon 6 is a synthetic polymer made from caprolactam and is known for its excellent abrasion resistance and low moisture absorption. On the other hand, Nylon 66 is made from the reaction of adipic acid and hexamethylenediamine and offers superior strength and heat resistance. Both Nylon 6 and Nylon 66 are widely used in various industries such as fishing, textiles, and industrial applications due to their durability and versatility.</p></p>
