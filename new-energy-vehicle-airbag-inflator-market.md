<p><p>New Energy Vehicle Airbag Inflator Market includes three main types: Pyrotechnic Inflator, Stored Gas Inflator, and Hybrid Inflator. Pyrotechnic inflators use a small pyrotechnic charge to rapidly inflate the airbag. Stored Gas Inflators store compressed gas which is released into the airbag when triggered. Hybrid Inflators combine both pyrotechnic and stored gas systems for improved performance and safety. Each type of inflator has its own unique advantages and is utilized in different vehicle airbag systems based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
