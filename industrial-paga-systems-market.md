This type of system is limited in terms of scalability and flexibility. </p><p>On the other hand, Network Broadcasting Systems utilize digital technology to broadcast messages across multiple locations through a network. This type of system offers more advanced features such as centralized control, synchronized messaging, and remote access. Network Broadcasting Systems are considered to be more efficient and versatile compared to Traditional Pressure Broadcasting Systems.</p></p>
