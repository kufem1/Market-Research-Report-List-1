<p><p>The Flexible Screen market consists of various types, with PMOLED and AMOLED being two popular technologies. PMOLED (Passive-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) screens use a simpler design and are typically more cost-effective, making them suitable for small displays like wearables. On the other hand, AMOLED (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) screens offer better image quality and are commonly found in high-end smartphones and TVs. Both technologies contribute to the growing demand for flexible and innovative display solutions in the market.</p></p>
