<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) market consists of four main types: Column Assist EPS, Single-Pinion Assist EPS, Dual-Pinion Assist EPS, and Rack Assist EPS. Column Assist EPS utilizes a steering column to provide assistance, Single-Pinion Assist EPS uses a single-pinion system, Dual-Pinion Assist EPS employs two pinions for improved performance, and Rack Assist EPS integrates assistance directly into the steering rack. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different vehicle requirements and driving preferences.</p></p>
