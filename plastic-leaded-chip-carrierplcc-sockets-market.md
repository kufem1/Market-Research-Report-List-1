<p><ul><li>THT</li><li>SMT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic Leaded Chip Carrier (PLCC) sockets are available in two main types - Through Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT). THT sockets involve the components being inserted and soldered into holes on the circuit board, while SMT sockets are affixed directly onto the surface of the board. THT sockets are known for their robustness and reliability, making them suitable for harsh environments. On the other hand, SMT sockets offer space-saving benefits and improved electrical performance, making them ideal for high-density applications.</p></p>
