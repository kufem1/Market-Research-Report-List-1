<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) products are categorized into three main types: foam, elastomer, and others. Foam TPU products are lightweight and have excellent cushioning properties, making them ideal for applications such as footwear and padding. Elastomeric TPU products offer high elasticity and durability, making them suitable for products like seals, gaskets, and conveyor belts. The "Others" category includes TPU products with unique properties and applications, such as TPU films for packaging and TPU coatings for textiles.</p></p>
