<p><p>Activated carbon from agricultural waste is produced in two main forms - powder and particles. Particles of activated carbon are larger and are typically used in gas phase applications like air filtration. Both forms are effective in removing impurities and contaminants from various sources, and their market demand continues to grow due to their eco-friendly and sustainable nature.</p></p>
