<p><p>The tobacco harvester market consists of two main types: self-propelled tobacco harvesters and walk-behind tobacco harvesters. Self-propelled tobacco harvesters are large machines that are powered by an engine and are capable of harvesting tobacco on a larger scale. Walk-behind tobacco harvesters, on the other hand, are smaller machines that are operated manually and are ideal for smaller-scale tobacco farming operations. Both types of harvesters are designed to efficiently harvest tobacco leaves, but cater to different sizes of farms and levels of mechanization.</p></p>
