<p><ul><li>DIP Type</li><li>SMD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Crystal Products market comprises two main types: DIP (Dual Inline Package) and SMD (Surface Mount Device) types. DIP type quartz crystal products are designed with through-hole technology, where the pins are mounted on a circuit board by inserting them into corresponding holes. SMD type quartz crystal products, on the other hand, utilize surface mount technology, where they are directly mounted on the surface of the circuit board. Both types serve as essential components in electronic devices, providing precise timing and frequency control for various applications.</p></p>
