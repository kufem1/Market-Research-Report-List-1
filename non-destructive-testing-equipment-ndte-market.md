<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing Equipment (NDTE) market includes various types of equipment used for inspecting materials and components without causing damage. Ultrasonic testing equipment uses sound waves to detect flaws, Magnetic particle testing equipment checks for surface cracks, Visual inspection equipment involves visual examination, Radiography testing equipment uses X-rays, Penetrant testing equipment identifies surface defects, Eddy current testing equipment detects flaws in conductive materials, and Acoustic emission testing equipment monitors for stress and material integrity. Each type of equipment plays a vital role in ensuring the quality and safety of industrial components.</p></p>
