<p><strong>The Opacimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Paper Opacimeter</li><li>Transmissive Opacimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Opacimeters are devices used to measure the opacity of gases and liquids, particularly in the automotive and environmental industries. Filter Paper Opacimeters utilize a filter paper to physically block light passing through a sample, providing a direct measurement of opacity. On the other hand, Transmissive Opacimeters measure opacity by transmitting light through a sample and analyzing the amount of light that is absorbed or scattered. Both types of opacimeters are essential for maintaining air quality standards and optimizing combustion processes.</p></p>
