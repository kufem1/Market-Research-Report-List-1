<p><ul><li>On-premise</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Advertising agency software can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based.</p><p>On-premise software is installed and operated on the agency's local servers or systems. It provides complete control and data security but requires substantial upfront investment, ongoing maintenance, and limited accessibility outside the agency premises.</p><p>Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and remote accessibility. The agency pays for a subscription or usage model, eliminating the need for upfront costs and hardware management. Data is stored and secured by the service provider.</p></p>
