<p><p>A Video Management System (VMS) is a software solution used to manage and control video surveillance cameras and recordings. The VMS market can be classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based VMS operates on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage videos through the internet. It offers scalability, easy installation, and flexibility. On the other hand, on-premises VMS involves installing the system on local servers and requires physical infrastructure. It provides better security, control, and customization options but may have higher costs and limited accessibility.</p></p>
