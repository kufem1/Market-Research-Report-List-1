<p><p>The marine batteries market consists of various types, namely Gel Batteries, Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) Batteries, and Flooded Batteries. Gel Batteries employ a gel electrolyte, making them spill-proof and maintenance-free. AGM Batteries use a fiberglass mat for electrolyte absorption, providing high-performance, vibration resistance, and quick charging capabilities. Flooded Batteries, also known as wet cell batteries, utilize a liquid electrolyte and require regular maintenance. These batteries offer good power output and are cost-effective. Each type of battery caters to different needs and preferences in the marine industry.</p></p>
