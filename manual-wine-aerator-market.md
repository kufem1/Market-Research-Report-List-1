<p><ul><li>In Bottle Wine Aerator</li><li>Handheld Wine Aerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual wine aerator market includes two main types: in-bottle wine aerators and handheld wine aerators. In-bottle wine aerators are designed to attach directly to the bottle, allowing for easy and efficient aeration as the wine is poured. Handheld wine aerators are portable devices that are held above the glass while pouring, promoting aeration as the wine flows through the aerator. Both types of aerators serve the purpose of enhancing the flavor and aroma of wine by exposing it to oxygen before consumption.</p></p>
