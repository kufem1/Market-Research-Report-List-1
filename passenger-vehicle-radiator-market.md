<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Radiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aluminum Radiator</li><li>Copper Radiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger vehicle radiators come in two main types: aluminum radiators and copper radiators. Aluminum radiators are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer good heat transfer efficiency. They are commonly used in modern vehicles due to their durability and corrosion resistance. On the other hand, copper radiators are known for their high thermal conductivity, making them efficient at dissipating heat. While copper radiators are less common in modern vehicles, they are still used in some high-performance or specialty vehicles where superior heat transfer is crucial.</p></p>
