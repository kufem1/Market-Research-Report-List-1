<p><strong>The Steel Plant Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Type</li><li>Batch Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel plant furnaces can be categorized into two main types: continuous type and batch type. Continuous type furnaces are designed for continuous production processes, where materials are constantly fed into the furnace and products are continuously discharged. On the other hand, batch type furnaces are used for intermittent production processes, where materials are loaded in batches and the furnace is periodically emptied and reloaded. Each type offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the steel plant.</p></p>
