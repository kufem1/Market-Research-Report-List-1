<p><p>Thrombosis Viscoelastic Analysis System market includes two main types: Thromboelastography (TEG) and Rotational thromboelastometry (ROTEM). Both types are diagnostic tools used to assess the blood clotting process in patients. TEG measures the development and stability of blood clots, providing information on clotting time, clot strength, and clot dissolution. Similarly, ROTEM evaluates clot formation, stability, and breakdown, but it has added features that allow for the targeted administration of specific clotting factors. These systems assist healthcare professionals in diagnosing and managing various blood clotting disorders.</p></p>
