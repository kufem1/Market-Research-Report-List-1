<p><p>The orally dissolving film drug market includes two main types, namely, orodispersible film and oromucosal film. Orodispersible films rapidly dissolve in the mouth, providing quick and easy drug absorption through the oral mucosa. On the other hand, oromucosal films are designed to be placed in the oral mucosa for controlled drug release. Both types offer convenient and efficient drug delivery options, making them popular choices for patients who have difficulty swallowing traditional forms of medication.</p></p>
