<p><p>Vertical Integration in the Integrated Delivery Network (IDN) market refers to the consolidation of various healthcare services or organizations along a single continuum of care. This involves merging multiple entities such as hospitals, physician practices, and long-term care facilities under one ownership and management. Horizontal Integration, on the other hand, entails the joining of similar healthcare providers or facilities at the same level of care. It typically involves the merger or acquisition of hospitals or healthcare systems. Both types of integration aim to improve coordination, efficiency, and quality of care within the healthcare delivery system.</p></p>
