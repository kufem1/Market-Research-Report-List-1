<p><p>Endpoint Data Loss Prevention (DLP) is a security solution designed to protect sensitive data and prevent its unauthorized access, transfer, or unauthorized use outside the organization's network. These solutions can monitor and control data transfers through various channels, including email, USB drives, cloud storage services, and web applications.</p><p>The Endpoint DLP market is experiencing steady growth due to the increasing adoption of cloud-based services, the rise in cyber-attacks, and stringent data protection regulations.
