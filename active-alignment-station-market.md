<p><ul><li>4 DOF</li><li>5 DOF</li><li>6 DOF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active alignment stations are precision tools used in the manufacturing process to align optical components. They are categorized based on the number of degrees of freedom (DOF) they offer. 4 DOF stations provide movement in four directions, suitable for simpler alignment tasks. 5 DOF stations add an additional degree of freedom for more complex alignments. 6 DOF stations offer the most flexibility, allowing for movement in all six directions for the most precise alignments. Each type caters to different levels of alignment precision required in optical manufacturing processes.</p></p>
