<p><ul><li>Hard Ceramics</li><li>Soft Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric tiles are divided into two main types: hard ceramics and soft ceramics. Hard ceramic piezoelectric tiles are made of materials like lead zirconate titanate, which have high piezoelectric coefficients and are commonly used in applications where high power output is required. Soft ceramic piezoelectric tiles, on the other hand, are made of materials like polyvinylidene fluoride, which have lower piezoelectric coefficients but are more flexible and can be easily integrated into various structures. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
