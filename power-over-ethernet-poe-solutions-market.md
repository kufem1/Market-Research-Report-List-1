<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) Solutions market consists of two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controllers & ICs and Powered Device (PD) Controllers & ICs. PSE Controllers & ICs are components that allow network devices to supply power to PoE-enabled devices, such as IP cameras or wireless access points. On the other hand, PD Controllers & ICs enable devices to receive power from the network infrastructure. Both types play a crucial role in enabling the deployment of PoE technology in various applications.</p></p>
